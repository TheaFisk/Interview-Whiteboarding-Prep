\# Overview



I want to better prepare for LeetCode style interview coding problems? 



I got you.



A while ago, I did an interview with a company I very much wanted to work for. I was still taking some pretty intermediate college programming courses, and I had never done a technical interview before. It was a miracle they even wanted to interview me. 



I got to the interview, crossing my fingers that they wouldn't ask me to do anything technical. 



"Now we would like to switch to some live programming problems. If you could share your screen and pull up Notepad-"



I immediately panicked. I forgot everything I knew and abandoned all reason. I pulled up notepad and the request was said: 



Make a linked list in C. 



All cards on the table, I had no idea what a linked list was. I was scrambling, trying to pretend like I knew what was going on. I feebly typed some words and they asked if I knew what pointers were. 

I didn't. 





It was the most embarrassing moment of my life. 



At that moment, I resolved. I wasn't going to give up. 



That's what this is for. I'm collecting all the leetcode-style problems I can find and running through logic \& solutions. 



If you know of a problem that I'm missing or run into something you want to see done, reach out and I am more than happy to add it!
